Champ Home Care LLC is committed to ensuring the safety, comfort, and independence of your loved ones through high-quality, customized home care services. Serving families across Wiese Way, Sacramento, and Santa Rosa, California, we specialize in tailor-made care plans designed to meet the unique needs of each individual in the comfort of their own home.

Promote Independence

We tailor our services to find the right balance between support and independence for our clients by assisting them physically as well as boosting their self-esteem, preserving their dignity and improving their overall quality of life.

Ensure Safety and Comfort

What We Offer?

Deserve Our Best Services

What we do has a far greater impact than what we say! At Champ Home Care LLC, we firmly believe that affordable in-home care services in California are built on compassion, trust, and dedication.

Service 01

Dignified Personal Care

Bathing, grooming, oral care, hygiene, dressing, toileting, meal preparation, medication reminders and general maintenance.

Service 02

Companion Care

Provides clients with conversation, reminiscing, hobbies, recreational, exercise and other activities.

Service 03

Respite Care

Short-term relief for primary caregivers. Flexible cover from a few hours to several days and weeks at a time.

Service 04

Dementia Care & Hospice Support

Specialized care for seniors dealing with dementia, Alzheimer's, Parkinson's, stroke and cancer, including post-hospitalization and end of life support.

Service 05

Transportation & Mobility

Arrangement of safe, reliable non-medical transportation to appointments, events and activities with care and compassion.

Service 06

24-Hour Care

Round-the-clock professional care and supervision for clients who need continuous support and assistance throughout the day and night.
